Distance Learning Programs

WINGS provides guidance and academic support for students who wish to complete their education through open and distance learning systems. Our programs are designed for working students, dropouts, homemakers, and learners who require flexible study options.

Boards We Provide Support For

NIOS

National Institute of Open Schooling (Government of India body) offering Secondary and Senior Secondary education through open mode.

MP State Open School (MPSOS)

State-level open schooling system under Madhya Pradesh authorities for students requiring flexible education.

Vidarbha Madhyamik Shiksha Mandal

Alternative academic pathway for students re-entering education streams.

Jamia Urdu Aligarh

Board offering Urdu-medium and alternative education streams for eligible students.

Who Can Apply?

  • Students who left school midway
  • Working professionals
  • Married women resuming education
  • Students preparing for competitive exams
  • Those needing flexible study timing

Flexible Study Options

  • Evening Classes
  • Weekend Support Sessions
  • Online Guidance
  • Printed + Digital Study Material
  • Exam Preparation Assistance
Important Note:
Recognition, eligibility for higher education, and government employment depend on the respective board regulations and official recruitment rules. Students are advised to verify eligibility as per their academic and career goals.
